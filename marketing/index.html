Usage Notes

Headers use 100% line-height with -2% letter-spacing for tight, impactful display.

Labels use 1% letter-spacing for improved readability at small sizes.

Sub headers and body text use 140% line-height for comfortable reading.

Header color (#094A27) provides strong contrast against light backgrounds.

Label color (#296A3C) differentiates labels from headers visually.

Body color (#3C3F44) ensures readability without harsh contrast.

Logo Guidelines

Clear SpaceMinimum clear space equal to the height of the 'M' in McGill on all sides.
Minimum SizeNever smaller than 1 inch (print) or 120px wide (digital).
Color UsageOnly use the four approved versions. Never alter logo colors.
BackgroundsPrimary on light. Reversed/white on dark or green backgrounds.
Don'tsNever stretch, rotate, add effects, or place on busy backgrounds.

05 / Trust Signal Placement

Strategic placement of trust signals on the ecommerce site to build customer confidence at key decision points in the buying journey.

Visual Hierarchy

Tier 1 (ISO, AS9100D) = largest, most prominent. Tier 2 (NAHAD, HSI) = standard. Tier 3 (Partner logos) = supporting. Never mix tiers at the same visual weight.

Homepage Hero / Above the Fold

ISO 9001 + AS9100D badges in a trust bar below the hero. The #1 trust signal for industrial buyers. Include NAHAD founding member badge in the same row.

Impact: High — First impression, sets credibility immediately

Product Detail Pages Below Product Specs

Show relevant certifications per product category. Aerospace products feature AS9100D prominently. Sanitary/PTFE products highlight FDA/3A compliance.

Impact: Critical — This is where purchase decisions happen

Category Pages Sidebar or Sub-header

Partner badges (Aeroquip Platinum, Continental) near the top of category pages for those product lines. Reinforces authorized distributor status.

Impact: Medium — Validates product authenticity

Footer (Global) Trust Bar

All Tier 1 and Tier 2 badges in a clean horizontal row. Persistent across every page.

Impact: Baseline — Constant reassurance

About / Company Dedicated Section

Full presentation with descriptions, cert numbers, and download links for cert documents. The deep-dive for procurement teams.

Impact: High — Procurement teams check here

Cart / Checkout Reassurance Strip

Small trust badges (ISO + AS9100D) near the CTA button. Reduces friction at the final decision point.

Impact: Critical — Reduces cart abandonment

Footer Badge Strip | Single-Color Variants

Monochrome badges in #10312B (Dark Green) for footer trust bars, invoices, and low-color contexts.
